Chicken Curry

Description:

A spicy, rich, flavourful and diverse Indian dish which consists of a chicken curry sauce base served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp medium spice paste
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 100g Greek yogurt
  • 1 small bunch of coriander, leaves chopped

Recipe:

  1. In a pan, fry the onion until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, cooking for a further minute.
  3. Chop the chicken into chunky pieces, add to the pan and fry for 5 mins
  4. Stir through the spice paste and tomatoes, along with 250ml water.
  5. Bring to the boil, lower to a simmer and cook on a gentle heat uncovered for 25-30
  6. Stir though the yogurt and season appropriately.
  7. Serve with fluffy basmati rice.
