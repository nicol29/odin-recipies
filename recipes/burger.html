Burgers

Description:

A huge single or triple burger with all the fixings, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions and special sauce or mayonnaise!

Ingredients:

  • Â½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion finely chopped
  • 500g Mince beef
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 4 slices mature Cheddar
  • 4 Burger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup to serve

Recipe:

  1. Add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened. Set aside.
  2. Combine the beef mince with the egg. Season, add the onions and mix well.
  3. Using your hands, shape into 4 patties.
  4. Cook the burgers for 5-6 minutes on each side.
  5. While the second side is cooking, lay a slice of cheese on top to melt.
  6. Lightly toast the buns in the pan.
  7. Fill with the lettuce, burgers and tomato slices.
